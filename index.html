Champbot-2015

A Champ-like ROV, with brief submersion capability, designed to meet the demands of the Champlain Mini Maker Faire's ChampBot Challenge of 2015

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Looking part Tanystropheus, part Gungan and sporting a wicked pair of drive-units on a pivoting boom, this bot is nearing completion.

The head is made of a license plate and the eyes are made of ping-pong balls lit by red LEDs. There are parts from several appliances, including a dehumidifier.

Specifications

  • Planned Launch September 12, 2015 in Arrowhead Lake, East Georgia Vt.
  • Type: Competition ROV, ChampBot

Key Features

  • Dual Hull, Over/Under, joined by 4 stanchions
  • Dual Drive Units which support piruett turning
  • Pivoting drive boom (propulsion/rotary-dive-plane selection)
  • Jaw control

Dimensions

  • Length: 125 cm
  • Beam: 70 cm
  • Draft: 50 cm (est)
  • Displacement Weight 8.2 kg

Main Propulsion

  • Two Johnson Electric motors with B&D reduction gears and chucks
  • Power: 0.6 kW (0.8 HP) (estimated)
  • Propellers: 23 cm diameter, 10 cm pitch
  • Max Speed: 2 kph (1.08 knots) (estimated)

Power

  • 5 Ah, 12.6 V AGM

Armament

  • 1x 6.35 mm butane igniter, burst fire (20% duty-cycle)
