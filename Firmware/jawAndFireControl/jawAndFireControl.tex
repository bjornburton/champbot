\section{Summary}
This will heat the igniter, delay until hot and open the gas valve for some maximum time.

\section{Heating}
The heating element is a tungsten head-light bulb.
It has a higher melting point than constantan or even Nichrome.
With that, since butane has a low autoignition temperature of 288\degc, the operating window should be very wide.
The heavy bulb filiment should resist cooling.
The PWM will need to to keep it a dull yellow for a couple seconds.

\section{Gas}
Gas will be controlled by it's container's valve, operated with a 12 Volt servo.
This may be a simple solenoid. 
Maximum time will be limited to minimize risk and waste.

\section{Jaw}
Since the mouth needs to be open, mouth control will be here too.
This will be a door-lock servo.
A spring will hold it closed and the servo will open it against the spring.
