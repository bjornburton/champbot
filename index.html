Champbot-2015

A Champ-like ROV, with brief submersion capability, designed to meet the demands of the Champlain Mini Maker Faire's ChampBot Challenge of 2015

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Looking part Tanystropheus, part Gungan and sporting a wicked pair of drive-units on a pivoting boom, this bot is nearing completion.

The head is made of a license plate and the eyes are made of ping-pong balls lit by red LEDs.
