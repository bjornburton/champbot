ChampBot-2016

A Champ-like ROV, with brief submersion capability, designed to meet the demands of the Champlain Mini Maker Faire's ChampBot Challenge of 2015

    Looking part Tanystropheus, part Gungan and sporting a wicked pair of drive-units on a pivoting boom, this bot is Done!

    The head is made of a license plate and the eyes are made of ping-pong balls lit by red LEDs. There are parts from several appliances, including a dehumidifier, computer-monitor, power-drills and various building materials.

    The design is far from any boat or sub, having more in common with a compact, mobile buoy. It would be difficult to have less consideration for hydrodynamics, aside from raising up from the water when under-way.

    Specifications

    • Launched on September 12, 2015 in Arrowhead Lake, East Georgia Vt.
    • Type: Competition ROV, ChampBot

    Construction Features

    • Dual Hull, Over/Under, joined by 4 stanchions
    • Dual Drive Units on a pivoting drive-boom
    • Microwave spread-spectum remote for an improved look (NEW for 2016)
    • MOSFET dual motor drive with electronic braking (Improved for 2016)
    • Radius computer with providing single-stick control and piruett turning
    • Autonymous dive including DDC control loop to maintain precise depth for an exact time (NEW for 2016)
    • Rotary Dive-Planes for rapid diving
    • Jaw control for an "authentic" float drop
    • Fire from the mouth, just as it should be

    Payload

    • 1x float, 20 cm from water surface

    Dimensions

    • Length: 96 cm
    • Beam: 70 cm
    • Draft: 30 cm (flank), 40 cm (stopped)
    • Height: 83 cm
    • Displacement Weight 9.2 kg

    Main Propulsion

    • 2x Johnson Electric motors with B&D reduction gears and chucks
    • Power: 0.6 kW (0.8 HP) (estimated)
    • Propellers: 2x, 23 cm diameter, 10 cm pitch
    • Max Speed: 3 kph (1.62 knots) (estimated)

    Power

    • 5 Ah, 12.6 V AGM
      • 30 Amp, self-resetting breaker

    Armament

    • 1x 100+ kW PFI (Projected Flame Igniter) (Improved output for 2016)
      • 70/30 Butane/Propane at 2.6 Bar (NEW for 2016)
      • Water-heated, large-surface evaporator (NEW for 2016)
      • Multi-spark, capacitive discharge pre-ignitor (NEW for 2016)
      • 5 mm orifice with guard horn (NEW for 2016)
      • 30 second capacity (3.6 MJ)
